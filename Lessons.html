CHOREOGRAPHERS:



  • In just two years as a choreographer, Colline Caube has shown remarkable talent and vision in the world of dance. Their ability to turn music into movement and ideas into captivating performances sets them apart.

  • Whether training beginners or polishing advanced dancers, they bring patience, creativity, and an infectious energy to every rehearsal.

  • Their choreography doesn’t just entertain—it tells stories, inspires performers, and leaves lasting impressions on audiences.
  • Leejung Lee is a South Korean choreographer and dancer known for her work with The Black Label and her appearances on "Street Woman Fighter".

  • Leejung has choreographed for various artists, including Lisa of Blackpink's solo debut. She also collaborated with V of BTS on dance videos after his military discharge.

  • Leejung emphasizes that dance is her "source of life" and that she draws inspiration from various sources to create unique and engaging choreography.
  • Nick Joseph is a choreographer, dancer, and creative director based in Los Angeles, California.

  • Nick has conducted numerous dance classes and workshops, sharing his expertise with aspiring dancers on a global scale, teaching in countries like China, Korea, Japan, Germany, India, and others.

  • Nick has expanded his creative scope traversing mediums working alongside Anderson .Paak on his debut film, “K-Pops!”, as lead choreographer.


  • Bada Lee is a renowned South Korean choreographer known for her work with top K-pop artists and her unique "swag" dance style.

  • She's gained recognition for choreographing for groups like NCT, aespa, and Kai, and her work often goes viral with popular dance challenges. She also leads the dance team BEBE, who won the "Street Woman Fighter 2" competition.

  • She has made choreographies for artists such as AESPA (Next Level/Savage/Drama/Girls), NCT (90’s Love/DejaVu/ZOO), WAYV (Action Figure/ On My Youth), Kai,(Rover/Peaches/Reason) BLACKPINK’ Lisa (LILI’s FILM The Movie), TRI.BE (Mire Prologue Film), Shownu X TEN (Special Collab Stage), The Boyz (Maverick), VeriVery (Undercover) and many more.
